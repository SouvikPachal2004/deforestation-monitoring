About ForestGuard

Our mission to protect the world's forests through technology

Monitoring Forests for a Sustainable Future

ForestGuard is an advanced deforestation monitoring system that leverages satellite imagery, artificial intelligence, and machine learning to detect and measure forest cover changes in near real-time.

Satellite Imagery Analysis

High-resolution satellite imagery processing with advanced computer vision algorithms.

AI-Powered Detection

Machine learning models trained to identify deforestation patterns with high accuracy.

Time-Series Analysis

Track forest cover changes over time to identify trends and predict future deforestation.

Real-Time Alerts

Immediate notifications when deforestation activities are detected in monitored areas.
